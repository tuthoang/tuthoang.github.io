Calorie Predictor

A part of a team that created an Android application that used Machine Learning to predict the calories of a type of food (burgers). We web scraped images for fast food burgers and labeled the calories according to the restaurant's nutrional facts on their website. We passed the images into a pre-trained MobileNet CNN for feature extraction. We then applied Principle Component Analysis and Linear regression on the extracted features. The necessary weights were saved onto the Android application and applied the necessary transformations onto input images from the user.
